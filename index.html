About

Not Your Typical Path. Exactly By Design.

I grew up playing hockey in Fort Collins — drafted 3rd overall in the USHL, won a Clark Cup, then took that competitive drive to West Point, where I captained the Division I team while finishing in the top 17% of my class. From there, six years flying Black Hawks and commanding people and missions across the Asia-Pacific.

Now I'm in the business world, and the same things that made me effective on the ice and in the Army are making me effective here. At Oply, I've generated over 50% of company revenue and expanded our reach from 13 to 48 states. The tools are different. The mentality isn't.

My target is the intersection of sports, private equity, and technology — because I've lived the athlete experience, operated in high-stakes environments, and know how to build from scratch. That combination is rare. I intend to use it.
